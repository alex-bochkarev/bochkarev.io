5 On refs in general

  • software to manage refs data (surprise!) search and organize data, produce citations and bibliography
  • data + cite style –> document
  • can have a server/sync/social components – if you need these.
  • available software:
    • huge comparison in the Wiki
    • usually there's a guide from the library. The university might have subscriptions / support.
    • look what is used in the lab!
    • numerous comparisons and discussions on the web (see reddit, quora, etc.)
    • I would mention:
      • Zotero, JabRef;
      • plus Mendeley, Refworks(Clemson guide), EndNote if we allow for commercial products
      • (maybe Papers – don't really know about Mac)
      • not an exhaustive list!
  • Requirements might be different – find the match!
